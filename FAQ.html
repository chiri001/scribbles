Is a resume same thing as a CV?

No. However we do both!
A resume is typically 1-2 pages in length and focuses on highlighting key qualifications and accomplishments. A CV is a comprehensive and detailed document that provides an extensive overview of a person's academic background, research, publications, presentations, and professional experience.
